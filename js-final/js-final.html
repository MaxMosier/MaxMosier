How to Use:

On a computer, click and drag your mouse to leave behind a trail of "attractors", which the "entities" will follow! By default, this trail is visible (and as you can see slowly fades away).

When entities get near one another, they begin turning to match the direction of others around them. By default, this happens quite slowly. The Entity-Entity Influence slider controls the strength of this effect. At 100%, this will result in a lot of clustering, while at 0%, the entities won't form "flocks."

The Randomize button randomly moves and rotates all existing entities.

Finally, the -10 Entities and +10 Entities buttons either add or remove 10 entities. The entity count is capped at 750, and has a minimum of 10.
